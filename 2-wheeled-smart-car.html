2 Wheeled Smart Car

A fun and educational robot that combines edge detection, object avoidance, and line following into one compact smart car build.

Project Layout

This project uses:

  • ESP32 microcontroller
  • HC-SR04 Ultrasonic sensor
  • 2x TCRT500 IR sensors
  • 3x 18650 batteries
  • 18650 Battery Holder
  • L298N motor driver
  • Modified Smart Robot Car Chassis Kit from Amazon
  • Mini 5V Buck Converter
  • 2x Metal Gear TT DC Motors
