About Us

DataConnect Robotics is where creativity meets engineering—and at the heart of it all is me, Nick Davidson, a lifelong tinkerer and problem solver. My journey started with a deep curiosity about how things work, which led me to study physics and data analytics. Along the way, I fell in love with the hands-on world of electronics and coding, picking up new skills every time I took something apart or built a project from scratch.

That’s why I’m developing a series of classes for kids and adults ages 8 and up—sharing what I’ve learned and hopefully sparking that same excitement in others. Whether you want to build your first robot, dive into the logic behind circuits, or just see what’s possible with a few simple parts, I’m here to help.

As I document each new robot design and data project, I’m inviting you along for the ride. DataConnect Robotics is more than a collection of projects—it’s a place to learn, experiment, and discover together.

At DataConnect Robotics, we don’t just talk about STEM—we roll up our sleeves and build it, one circuit and line of code at a time.
